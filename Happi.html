Happi

If you're Happy and you know it, Happi it!!!

Record and elevate your happiness with Happi, an easy-to-use app that provides an aesthetic account of all your happiest memories. Simply, tap the smiley face and capture your HappiMoment!!!!

Happi encourages users to tap the yellow smiley face at any HappiMoment during the day! Each HappiMoment is recorded and entered into a graph, which is updated every week. Users can see a visual of three important factors:

  • When they are the happiest
  • what makes them happiest (what they love to do, eat, spend time with, etc.)
  • Their happiness level

    • By recognizing these happiness factors, users can enhance their daily life just by doing what makes them happy. Happi recommends checking in hourly to deliver the best results for your well-being:

  • Cope better with stress
  • Build self-confidence
  • Laugh more, live longer
